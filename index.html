About

SETI-Gathering is a festival to bring people together to have intense and beautiful experiences, be it on the dancefloors, with different kinds of musical delights, or in the surrounding areas enjoying the life in art, food and other revelations. Driven by the wish to create a festival focused on the darker and more experimental side of Psy- and Tekno-culture we are trying to create a space for everybody to free themselves. We would like to invite you joining us on the next edition of SETI to kick-off the festival-season.

SETI 2k22

This years edition of SETI-Gathering will happen in a scaled down size. We will make a 36h party in a beautiful location close to Erfurt. We will host 2 Floors with mostly dark and psychedelic music. The event will be happening on the 27th of may.

location
SETI 2k22 will happen in a beautiful location next to Erfurt. If you need more information on the location please subscribe to our newsletter. We will send out the location, shortly before the event will start.
Imortant notice:
We can not provide any camping space at the location. But probably you will find a good place to sleep in the surrounding area... ;) We will have a big Chillout area where you can also rest and refill your energy.

tickets

The recommended contribution towards expences will be 15 Euro paid at the gate.
